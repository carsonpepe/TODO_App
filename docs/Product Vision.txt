Group name: TODO Pro

Product Vision:
For busy individuals Who have a lot of things to do The TODO Pro is an interactive TODO list That easy to manage and clearly displays your TODO items. Unlike Microsoft TODO and Apple Reminders Our Product will not harvest your data and sell it to the highest bidder.
User Stories:

Carson Pepe:
As a user, I would like to be able to click a checkbox on a TODO item and see it grayed out
As a user, when I check off an item as “done”, I would like to see it under a different section on the UI titled “Completed”
As a user, I’d like to be able to look at different lengths of timelines for my TODO items based on their dates
